About Me...

I'm Steve Benedict, an aspiring professional web developer based in Orange County, California. I own and operate my own property management business. I am also a stay-at-home dad. I like creating new things. Whether it's a house, a computer program, a web page, or electronic music, I have always been creating.

I am passionate about technology and, especially, software development. Before starting a family and my own business, I worked as a software test lead and program manager for a major software company. I shipped several major software programs that many people have used.

The emergence of cryptocurrency sparked a my interest in JavaScript. After reading some books on the latest JavaScript advancements, I decided to enroll in a coding boot camp called LearningFuze, in Irvine.

At LearningFuze, it was like being back in the software development environment that I remember fondly from my previous time in the industry. For 12 weeks and up to 12 hours a day, I learned many new things about web development. It was a collaborative, fast-paced environment, and I built web applications with the latest technologies and techniques. Plus, there was free pizza!

Since then, I have decided that I want to kick-start a new career as a software developer. I am especially interested in focusing on server-side development, as it is an area where I believe I can apply my current skills and continue to challenge myself to grow as a programmer.
